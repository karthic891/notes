Where does eclipse deploy wars with Tomcat server

Server Path: .metadata/.plugins/org.eclipse.wst.server.core/tmp0
Deploy Path: wtpwebapps

How do I reload .bashrc without logging out and back in?

You just have to enter the command: source ~/.bashrc
or you can use the shorter version of the command: . ~/.bashrc
